## Business Value

### Operational Impact
This workflow eliminates the need for manual follow‑up when items are
assigned in the ETQ SharePoint tracking list. By automatically notifying
assigned users, the process reduces delays caused by missed or unclear
ownership and improves overall responsiveness.

Key operational improvements include:
- Immediate visibility of assignment changes
- Reduced reliance on manual communication
- Improved accountability for assigned items
- Faster action on newly created or updated records

### Data Quality & Governance
Ensuring that assigned users are notified promptly increases the
likelihood that items are actively reviewed and updated. This leads to:
- More accurate status updates
- Fewer stalled or forgotten records
- Improved reliability of operational data used in reporting

The workflow reinforces proper ownership, which is critical for
maintaining trustworthy downstream analytics.

### Analytics Enablement
By improving how assignment data is acted upon, this automation supports:
- More reliable cycle time and SLA tracking
- Cleaner inputs for KPI dashboards
- Better insight into workload distribution and bottlenecks

The workflow helps ensure that analytical metrics reflect real,
timely activity rather than delayed responses.

### Scalability & Maintainability
- Automatically applies to all new and updated items
- Requires no additional manual effort as volume increases
- Can be extended to include additional logic such as:
  - Status-based notifications
  - Escalation rules
  - Reminder workflows

### Skills Demonstrated
- Power Automate workflow design
- SharePoint Online integration
- Conditional logic and control flow
- Handling structured data fields (Person columns)
